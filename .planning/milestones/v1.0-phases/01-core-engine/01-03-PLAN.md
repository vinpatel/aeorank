    - h1 on &gt;80% pages, proper h1→h2→h3 sequential hierarchy → score 10

    - No pages with concise first paragraphs → score 0

    - First paragraph &lt;300 chars on 30-60% pages → score 6
    - First paragraph &lt;300 chars on &gt;60% pages → score 10

    - No FAQ schema or Q&amp;A patterns → score 0
    - FAQ sections exist but no schema → score 3
    - FAQPage schema present → score 6
    - FAQPage schema with 3+ Q&amp;A pairs → score 10

    Dimension 7 - eeat-signals (weight: medium):
    - No author, no About page, no dates → score 0
    - Has some author/date signals → score 4
    - Has author names + publication dates → score 7
    - Author names + dates + About page + external credentials → score 10

    Dimension 8 - meta-descriptions (weight: medium):
    - No meta descriptions → score 0
    - Meta desc on &lt;50% pages → score 4
    - Meta desc on 50-80% pages, length 50-160 chars → score 7
    - Meta desc on &gt;80% pages, all optimal length → score 10

    Dimension 9 - sitemap (weight: low):
    - No sitemap → score 0
    - Sitemap exists but no lastmod → score 5
    - Sitemap with lastmod, &gt;80% within 90 days → score 8
    - Sitemap with lastmod, &gt;80% within 30 days → score 10

    Dimension 10 - https-redirects (weight: low):
    - HTTP only, no HTTPS → score 0
    - HTTPS but with redirect chains (2+) → score 5
    - Clean HTTPS with canonical → score 10

    Dimension 11 - page-freshness (weight: low):
    - No date signals anywhere → score 0
    - Dates present but &gt;90 days old → score 3
    - Dates within 90 days on some pages → score 6
    - Dates within 30 days on &gt;50% pages → score 10

    Dimension 12 - citation-anchors (weight: medium):
    - No headings have id attributes → score 0
    - &lt;30% headings have ids → score 3
    - 30-60% headings have ids → score 6
    - &gt;60% of h2/h3 headings have id attributes → score 10
