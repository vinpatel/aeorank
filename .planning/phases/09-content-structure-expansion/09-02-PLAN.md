    - A page "disambiguates well" if: entity term appears in first paragraph AND appears 3+ times total in bodyText
    - Calculate ratio of well-disambiguated pages to total pages
    - Scoring: ratio > 0.6 = 10, > 0.4 = 7, > 0.2 = 4, > 0 = 2, else 0
    - Hint: "Define the primary entity early in each page and use consistent terminology throughout"
