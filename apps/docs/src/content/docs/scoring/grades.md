AEOrank converts your 0-100 score into a letter grade for quick assessment.

## Grade scale

| Grade | Score Range | Meaning |
|-------|-----------|---------|
| **A+** | 95-100 | Exceptional — your site is fully optimized for AI engines |
| **A** | 85-94 | Excellent — strong AI visibility across all criteria |
| **B** | 70-84 | Good — solid foundation with room to improve |
| **C** | 55-69 | Fair — basic AI signals present but significant gaps |
| **D** | 40-54 | Poor — most AI engines will struggle to understand your site |
| **F** | 0-39 | Failing — your site is effectively invisible to AI engines |

## What each grade means

### A+ (95-100)

Your site has all the signals AI engines look for. `llms.txt` is well-structured, schema markup is comprehensive, content is organized for AI consumption, and all crawlers have access. You're among the best-optimized sites.

### A (85-94)

Strong across all dimensions. Minor improvements possible (perhaps a missing FAQ schema or incomplete meta descriptions) but AI engines can find and cite your content effectively.

### B (70-84)

Good foundation. You likely have some schema markup and decent content structure, but may be missing `llms.txt`, FAQ markup, or citation anchors. Focus on high-weight dimensions for the biggest gains.

### C (55-69)

Basics are in place but significant gaps exist. Common pattern: decent content structure and meta descriptions, but no `llms.txt`, incomplete schema markup, and missing AI crawler directives. A focused effort can push you to B quickly.

### D (40-54)

Your site has some positive signals but AI engines will struggle. Likely missing most specialized files (`llms.txt`, FAQ blocks, citation anchors) and may have limited schema markup. Start with the [quick start guide](/getting-started/) to identify the biggest wins.

### F (0-39)

Your site is effectively invisible to AI engines. This is common for sites that haven't considered AI visibility at all. The good news: deploying AEOrank's generated files typically jumps you 20-40 points immediately.

## Typical improvements

| Starting Grade | After deploying files | Improvement |
|----------------|----------------------|-------------|
| F (20) | C-D (45-55) | +25-35 points |
| D (45) | B (70-75) | +25-30 points |
| C (60) | B-A (75-85) | +15-25 points |
| B (75) | A (85-90) | +10-15 points |

:::tip
Most sites see the biggest improvement from deploying `llms.txt` and `schema.json` — the two highest-weighted signals.
:::
