AI Visibility Scanner

AEOrank scans any website and scores its AI readability across 12 dimensions. Get a score from 0-100 and actionable recommendations to improve your visibility to AI assistants like ChatGPT, Claude, and Perplexity.

How It Works

Simply paste your URL and we'll crawl up to 50 pages, analyzing content structure, schema markup, and AI crawler access. The scan completes in under 30 seconds.

FAQ

What is AEO?

AEO stands for AI Engine Optimization — the practice of making your website more readable and citable by AI assistants.

How is the score calculated?

The AEO score is a weighted average of 12 dimensions covering content structure, schema markup, AI crawler access, and more.
